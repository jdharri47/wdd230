Target Audience

The target audience is as follows:

  • Who: Owners of local and international businesses who are looking to improve the local area through collaboration and planning.
  • Age: 18 and up
  • Access:The target audience will access this site mostly through mobile devices but also through desktops as well.
  • Income:Incomes will vary, however the target income will lie in the medium range.

Personas

Will Occaric

Will is a young business owner who is interested in growing his business. He uses this site to find opportunities to do so. He strives for honest ways in which he can grow and improve his dealings, and seeks those in the chamber for help to do so.

Pecy Altman

Percy is a middle aged man who is the founder of a local business. He seeks to improve the community through funding and hosting local events. He uses this site as a means to stay informed with what events are coming up for the community and how local buisnesses can participate to help.

Scenarios

  1. What local events are coming up?
  2. How may I join the Chamber of Commerce?
  3. How can my business participate in local events?
  4. How can my business help the local populace?
  5. What buisnesses are taking part in local events?
